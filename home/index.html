The Poop Print Project

Geneva's free fidget initiative: Turning 3D printer scraps into comfort for anxiety relief.

How It Works

My 3D printer leaves behind tiny filament scraps (we call them "poops" in the community). Instead of throwing them away, I transform these scraps into free fidget tools for Geneva residents.

As someone who understands how powerful fidgets can be in easing anxiety, the creator of The Poop Print Project saw a unique opportunity: transform 3D printing waste into free tools for comfort. What began as scraps from a personal printer has grown into a local initiative to bring relief and sustainability together in Geneva. Every fidget is made from material that would otherwise be discarded.
