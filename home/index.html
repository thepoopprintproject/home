The Poop Print Project

Geneva's free fidget initiative: Turning 3D printer scraps into comfort for anxiety relief.

How It Works

My 3D printer leaves behind tiny filament scraps (we call them "poops"). Instead of discarding them, I turn them into free fidget tools for Geneva residents.

Mission

Our mission is to reduce 3D printing waste, support mental well-being, and strengthen community bonds in Geneva.
