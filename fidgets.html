The Poop Print Project

Geneva's free fidget initiative: Turning 3D printer scraps into comfort for anxiety relief.

What We Create

Each fidget is unique, crafted from recycled filament scraps to provide sensory comfort. Designs are simple, tactile, and free to anyone in Geneva who could use a moment of calm.
