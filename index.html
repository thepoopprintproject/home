The Poop Print Project

Geneva's free fidget initiative: Turning 3D printer scraps into comfort for anxiety relief.

How It Works

My 3D printer leaves behind tiny filament scraps ("poops"). Instead of wasting them, I turn these scraps into free fidget tools for Geneva residents. No cost, no strings.

I'm a student – this is my way of giving back while reducing waste. Every fidget is made from your community's discarded material.
