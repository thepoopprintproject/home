My Story

This project was born out of a deep interest in 3D printing, sustainability, and mental health support. Through personal experience, I came to understand how effective fidget tools can be in managing anxiety and improving focus.

This project started when I noticed how much filament waste my 3D printer produced. Those small blobs and failed prints (we call them "poops" in the 3D printing community) were piling up, heading straight for the trash. Meanwhile, I was spending money on store-bought fidgets that weren't always affordable or effective.

That's when it clicked: Why not turn this waste into something useful for the community?

How I Ensure Quality & Safety

While these fidgets are made from "waste" material, I take safety seriously:

  • Non-toxic materials only: Made with safe PLA (biodegradable) and PETG (durable, skin-safe).
  • Smooth edges: Every fidget is checked for sharp points or rough surfaces
  • Clean workspace: All materials are handled in a clean, organized environment
  • Age considerations: Parents should supervise young children with any fidget tool
