About The Poop Print Project

Why I Do This

The Problem

Small 3D printers waste around 20% of their filament. Anxiety is a common challenge, including in Geneva. The Poop Print Project connects these two challenges in a positive way.

The Solution

By creating free fidget tools from printer waste, the project:

  • Reduces unnecessary landfill use
  • Supports mental health and daily well-being
  • Inspires others to see waste as a resource, not rubbish

My Story

The project started when I realized how much filament waste my 3D printer produced. Those "poops" could become useful fidgets for the community. Now every fidget is made with care from recycled material.
